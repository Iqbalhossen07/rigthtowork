    Frequently asked questions

    Do you support visa applications?+

    Yes. We work with UK-verified employers and can guide you on roles that provide visa sponsorship where available.

    How long does the process take?+

    On average, successful applicants secure a role within 7 days once documents are verified.

    What documents are required?+

    Typically a UK-ready CV, proof of identity, and right-to-work documents where applicable. You can upload securely from your phone.

    Are there any fees?+

    Applying is free for workers. Some roles may require compliance checks handled by the employer.

    Is my data protected?+

    Absolutely. We follow UK Data Privacy and GDPR requirements and use secure uploads.

    How can I get help?+

    Our support team is available 24/7 via chat and email. We also have a Help Centre.
